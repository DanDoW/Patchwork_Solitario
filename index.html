Solovariant I
Improve your personal high score!

Crown yourself Patchwork King

Setup

  1. Use the normal setup for 2-player-game, you are the start player.
  2. Use the second time token as a dummy player - he plats without buttons. His advance depends on the free spaces bet ween his and your time token
Free soaces
  • 0

    Kings time token is on top of yours or one space behind:

    Dummy player buys first patch.

  • 1

    Kings time token is two spaces behind:

  • 2

    Kings time token is three spaces behind:

  • 3+

    Kings will advance and you are next.

Dummy player does not need to pay for his patch, but he has to move his time token by a number of spaces as depicted on the label.

Scoring

  1. Determine the number of buttons you have left and add (if earned) the value of the special tile.
  2. Substract 2 points from your score for each empty space of your quilt board.

Solovariant II
Challenge the Patchwork Master!

Beat the Patchwork Master

Setup

  1. Use the normal game setup for 2-player-game, you are the start player.
  2. Place the special tile (7x7) in the center (last space) of the time board.
  3. Decide if you want to be the start player.
  4. The Patchwork Master moves his time token like in variant 1 additional some extra rules:
Extra rules

The Patchwork Master also starts with 5 buttons but he has to pay only the amount of buttons depicted on the label (this means the buttons wich count for you as button income). If he cannot afford any patch, he advances and recieve 1 button for each space he moves his time token.

Income

  1. You recieve a number of buttons acording to the patches on your quilt board (like in the normal rules).
  2. The Patchwork Master only gets button income, if he catches a special patch (he recieves a number of buttons according to the patches he has bought).
Special tile

the player who first reaches the center of the time board earns the special tile.

Scoring

  1. Determine the number of buttons you and the Patchwork Master have left.
  2. Add the value of the special tile to his or your score.
  3. Substract 2 points for each empty space of your quilt board from your score. Beat the Patchwork Master is aim of the game.

If it is too hard for you to beat the Patchwork Master use the same rules like in variant II and try another complexity factor as follows:

Complexity factors

Beginners

  1. Patchwork Master starts with 5 buttons, player with 10.
  2. Put on each of the first 3 special patches a "5 button" tile and on the last two only a "1 button" tile - the buttons are the price for getting the patch. The "1 button" tile on a special patch indicates that this special patch is used for the master as button income (if he takes it).
  3. In the end of the game every free space counts -1 buttons fot you.

Experienced

  1. Patchwork Master and player start with 5 buttons.
  2. Put on each of the first 2 special patches a "5 button" tile and on the last three only a "1 button" tile - the buttons are the price for getting the patch. The "1button" tile on a special patch indicates that this special patch is used for the Patchwork Master as button income (if he takes it).
  3. In the end of the game every free space counts -2 buttons for you.

Professionals

  1. Patchwork Master and player start with 5 buttons.
  2. Use the special patches without any button tiles on it, so each of them counts for the master as button income (if he takes it).
  3. In the end of the game every free space counts -3 buttons for you.
